# Destination Prompt Pack

Paste each prompt into Google Docs. Voice type your answer. Keep it quick and factual.

---

## GETTING THERE
How do you get here from Manila? From Cebu? Flight time, ferry time, cost in pesos.

## GETTING AROUND
Tricycle, motorbike, van? What do short trips cost?

## WHERE TO STAY
One budget, one mid-range, one splurge. Name, price per night in PHP and USD.

## WHERE TO EAT
Three real places you ate. Dish name, price in PHP. One must-order dish.

## TOP 3 ACTIVITIES
Three things to do. Cost, how long, how to book.

## DAILY BUDGET
Backpacker per day. Mid-range per day. Luxury per day. PHP and USD.

## BEST MONTHS
Best weather months. Rainy months to avoid. Peak vs off-season.

## ESSENTIALS
Nearest airport. Days needed. Family friendly yes/no. WiFi quality. Cash or card. Language.

## SKIP LIST
What's overhyped. What to avoid. Common tourist mistakes.
